Meditation for your body.

A conversational somatic guide. Close your eyes, talk, move gently — and in about fifteen minutes your body lets go of what it's been holding.

You've been practicing struggle.

Every workout you pushed through, every time you forced yourself to relax — it trained the same thing. Your nervous system got good at bracing. SomaticEcho is practice in the opposite direction: it leads you through ease, over and over, until letting go is the thing your body knows by heart.

How it works

Led by voice. Practiced in the body.

01

You talk.

Out loud, to Echo. No script, nothing to perform or push through.

02

Echo guides your body.

Listening to your breath and voice, it leads you through simple positions and small, slow movements — gentle, never strenuous — that let the tension release.

03

Your body lets go.

Eyes closed, in about fifteen minutes, the holding starts to release on its own.

Not meditation, not exercise — the calm of one and the ease the other promised, without the strain of either.

Remember what it felt like to move with joy?

You feel it the first time — lighter, looser. But the real change shows up in the ordinary moments: standing in line without bracing, getting down to play with your kids and back up like it's nothing, a mind that stays quieter through the day. Fifteen minutes a day, and ease stops being something you visit and becomes the way you live. As the bracing lets go, the strength, flexibility, and coordination it was quietly costing you come back — your body moving like one connected piece again. Give it a couple of weeks — you won't believe how easy and athletic you feel.

Why it works

Conversational AI. Nothing prerecorded.

Echo isn't a library of calming tracks. It's a conversational AI that talks with you, live — listening to your breath, your voice, and how you're responding, and generating every word on the spot to guide you toward release. A somatic guide engineered for exactly that, built on a proprietary model of how tension is held and how it lets go — the result of original research you won't find anywhere else. What it understands about your body is technically exact; what it says is shaped for your nervous system to receive, not your intellect.

Honest by design

A guide, not a guru.

Echo is AI, and it tells you so. It won't hype you, lecture you, or pretend to feel what you feel. It points; you walk. And it only ever remembers what you ask it to.
